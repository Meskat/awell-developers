## Awell Studio

The web application used by clinical and product experts to build clinical pathway definitions.

### Pathway definition

A pathway definition is a structured representation of the workflow behind clinical pathways. It describes everything that can or should happen in a pathway, from the moment it starts to when it is considered completed.

## Awell Orchestration

Awell Orchestration is the software that runs pathways based on pathway definitions created in Awell Studio. It automatically handles the variability encoded in pathway definitions for you by evaluating all conditional logic, activating and discarding branches, scheduling activities and assigning them to the right stakeholder and more.
