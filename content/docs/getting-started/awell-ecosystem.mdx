Our web application used by clinical and product experts to build clinical pathways with Awell Studio. A pathway built in Awell Studio is called a `pathway definition model`.

## Awell Orchestration

When a version of the pathway definition model is published, it becomes available in our Orchestration domain. Our Orchestrator has a GraphQL API interface to orchestrate or execute your clinical pathways and feed your patient and care team apps with the next steps, the next information to show, or the next data to capture.

Where the pathway definition model is a quite elaborate model, an orchestrated pathway is reduced to 2 main elements for you to interact with:
