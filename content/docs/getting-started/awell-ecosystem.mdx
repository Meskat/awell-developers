## Awell Studio

Our web application used by clinical and product experts to build clinical pathways with Awell Studio. Once a pathway is published, it becomes available in our Orchestration domain.

## Awell Orchestration

A GraphQL API to orchestrate your clinical pathways and feed your patient and care team apps with the next steps, the next information to show, or the next data to capture.
