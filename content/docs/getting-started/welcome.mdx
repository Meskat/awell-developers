The future of healthcare is digital and the pandemic accelerated this digitization. Where the first wave in digital health was all about digitizing the consultation, we know see that **digital health winners need to own the care pathway end-to-end**.

## The problem

Owning the care pathway end-to-end requires significant time and investment. **It takes on average 15 months and $425.000 to develop a first release of a digital health product**.

### Why does it take so long and so much resources?

Today, clinical & product experts spend time mapping their care flows in tools like Miro or sometimes even in Word, Excel, or PDFs. Software engineers then hardcode these flows in patient and care team apps which results leads in:

- Time and resources lost
- Poor agility and speed
- Low iteration frequency

## How Awell Health can help

### Build

Clinical and product experts build pathways with Awell Studio, our convenient drag & drop builder. This reduces the time and resources to build an executable pathway.

### Validate and publish

Manage pathway versions, validate them, and publish for go live. Accelerate the speed of iteration and improve your pathways continuously.

### Orchestrate

Use our APIs to orchestrate your workflows and feed your patient and care team apps with the next steps, the next information to show, or the next data to capture. We take care of table stakes functionality so you can focus on what makes you unique.
